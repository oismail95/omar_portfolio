Amazon

Sort Center - Warehouse Team Member

In June 2020, Omar started working at Amazon as a Warehouse Team Member, which is located in Hazelwood, Missouri. He scanned products on correct pallets based on zip code by using his scanner gun, did inbound, which he had to load and unload pallets in trucks, he was a waterspider, which he had to wrap pallets that are high enough to close with boxes stacked on lanes, he was a splitter, which he had to separate boxes based on the letter, he did Non-Con Inbound, which he had to deal with heavy boxes and scan them to correct pallets based on zip code, and he did problem solve, which he had to repair damaged and leaked boxes. His experience is motivating because it kept him to pace up on tasks he did and he learned tasks he never done before.
